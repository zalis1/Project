The Project Proposal

1. What is your website about?

I am from Istanbul, Turkey and I decided to make my website a travel website for Istanbul. I will be including history of Istanbul, best restaurants, best places to visit, best hotels, things to do and tips before travelling.

2. What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

My objective in the website is to educate people on Istanbul and provide information for people who want to travel to the city. I realized the lack of a good travel site for Istanbul. I will be providing information for a perfect travel to Istanbul from a local person's point of view.

3. Who is your target audience set?

 My target audience is whomever wants to and is planning to travel to Istanbul and people who want to learn more about the city.

4. What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I will be including history of Istanbul, best restaurants, best places to visit, best hotels, things to do and tips before travelling. In these pages I will be using many images and provide the information from a local person's point of view.
